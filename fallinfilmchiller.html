EFFICIENT COOLING SOLUTIONS

FALLING FILM CHILLER

The Chillplus Falling Film Chiller is designed to optimize your cooling processes by significantly reducing chilling time, which in turn boosts production efficiency and ensures a faster return on investment. It’s easy-to-clean and inspect design requires virtually no downtime, maintaining continuous operation. Utilizing advanced heat transfer technology, the Chillplus handles incoming fluid temperatures over 27°C, eliminating the need for a secondary heat exchanger and preventing freeze-up issues. The fully enclosed structure ensures a hygienic process by preventing product contamination. These features make the Chillplus ideal for industries like dairy and food processing, offering a reliable and efficient cooling solution.
